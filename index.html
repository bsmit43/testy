Frequently Asked Questions

What types of adventures do you offer?

We offer a wide range of adventures, including hiking, rock climbing, white water rafting, mountain biking, and guided nature tours. We cater to all skill levels, from beginners to experienced adventurers.

What is included in the price of a trip?

The price of a trip typically includes all necessary equipment, professional guides, transportation to and from the activity location, and in some cases, meals. Specific inclusions will be detailed in the trip description.

What should I bring on my adventure?

We recommend bringing comfortable hiking shoes, weather-appropriate clothing (layers are best), sunscreen, a hat, sunglasses, a water bottle, and a small backpack. A detailed packing list will be provided for each specific trip.

Are your guides certified and experienced?

Yes, all of our guides are highly trained, certified, and experienced in their respective fields. They are passionate about adventure and dedicated to ensuring your safety and enjoyment.

What is your cancellation policy?

Our cancellation policy varies depending on the trip. Generally, cancellations made more than 30 days prior to the trip date will receive a full refund. Cancellations made within 30 days may be subject to a partial refund or credit towards a future trip. Please refer to the specific trip details for our cancellation policy.

Do you offer group discounts?

Yes, we offer discounts for groups of 6 or more people. Please contact us for more information and to discuss your group's needs.

Is travel insurance required?

While not strictly required, we highly recommend purchasing travel insurance to protect yourself against unforeseen circumstances such as trip cancellations, medical emergencies, or lost luggage.

How do I book an adventure?

You can book an adventure directly through our website or by contacting our customer service team. We are happy to assist you in choosing the perfect adventure for your needs and skill level.
